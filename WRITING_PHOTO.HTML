The Master Template

1

Introduction (The Anchor)

Describe the main action immediately.

Template: "In the picture, [Subject] is [Action]... "
Better: "This image is dominated by [Subject] who appears to be..."
2

Setting & Context

Where are they? What is the atmosphere?

Template: "[Subject] is in a [Location]. It looks [Adjective]."
Better: "The setting appears to be a [Location], characterized by [Detail] and [Lighting]."
3

Emotions & Interactions

How do they feel? How do they relate?

Template: "They look happy."
Better: "Their expressions exude a sense of [jollity/focus], suggesting a moment of..."
4

Conclusion (Speculation)

Add a final thought or prediction.

Template: "Overall, it is a nice picture."
Better: "Overall, the atmosphere seems [Adjective], implying that [Prediction]."
"In the picture, I see a group of students sitting at desks. They are studying and engaged in a lesson. The classroom has windows letting natural light in. Some students appear focused, while others are discussing. Overall, the atmosphere seems calm and educational."
