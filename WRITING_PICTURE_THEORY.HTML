The Final Master Template (150+ Score)

This is the refined version of the Universal Template. Memorize this exact structure.

“This well-composed image captures a scene set in [General Setting], with the main focus on [Primary Subject]. In the foreground, [Subject A] is [Action + Adverb], while in the background, [Secondary Subject/Environment] can be observed. The surrounding elements, such as [Specific Detail], help provide additional context. Judging by [Visual Evidence], it appears that [Logical Inference], creating a sense of [Atmosphere/Mood].”

THE "SINGLE OBJECT" TEMPLATE

(Use for: A chair, a phone, a fruit, a shoe, a bottle)

“This minimalistic image clearly shows a single [Object Name] resting on [Surface/Background]. The object is characterized by its [Adjective: e.g., smooth/rough/bright] texture and [Color] appearance. Lighting hits the [Top/Side] of the item, creating a sharp contrast against the simple surroundings. Judging by its isolation and condition, it appears to be [Inference: e.g., brand new/recently used/a display item], creating a sense of [Mood: Simplicity/Functionality].”

THE "FLAT DOCUMENT/SIGN" TEMPLATE

(Use for: A map, a street sign, a graph, a handwritten note, a logo)

“This image illustrates a clear [Type: Sign/Map/Chart] designed to convey specific information. The central text or symbol reads ‘[Insert Text or Describe Symbol]’, which is prominently displayed in [Color/Font Style]. The layout is [Adjective: Organized/Bold/Simple], utilizing [Design Element: e.g., Arrows/Colors] to guide the viewer's attention. Based on the content, this visual is likely intended for [Context: e.g., Drivers/Students/Tourists], serving the purpose of [Purpose: Warning/Direction/Instruction].”

THE "CLOSE-UP PORTRAIT" TEMPLATE

(Use for: A zoomed-in face, a selfie, a headshot where the background is invisible)

“This intimate close-up shot focuses entirely on the face of [Subject: e.g., a young woman/an elderly man]. Their expression is [Adjective: Cheerful/Serious/Pensive], characterized by [Detail: e.g., a bright smile/deep wrinkles/intense eyes]. The background is [Blurred/Solid/Minimal], ensuring that no distractions take away from the subject's features. Judging by [Visual Cue: e.g., the soft lighting/their relaxed muscles], the person appears to be feeling [Emotion], creating a sense of [Mood: Human connection/Intensity/Joy].”

Example 1: A Busy City Street (Urban)

“This well-composed image captures a scene set in a bustling metropolitan district, with the main focus on the heavy flow of traffic. In the foreground, several pedestrians are crossing the street hurriedly, while in the background, towering skyscrapers can be observed. The surrounding elements, such as bright digital billboards, help provide additional context. Judging by the overcast sky and wet pavement, it appears that it has recently rained, creating a sense of urgent daily routine.”

Example 2: A Library or Classroom (Academic)

“This well-composed image captures a scene set in a quiet academic environment, with the main focus on a group of students. In the foreground, a young woman is reading intensely, while in the background, rows of filled bookshelves can be observed. The surrounding elements, such as laptops and open notebooks, help provide additional context. Judging by their focused expressions, it appears that they are preparing for an upcoming exam, creating a sense of concentration and diligence.”

Example 3: A Park or Nature Scene (Landscape)

“This well-composed image captures a scene set in a serene natural park, with the main focus on a winding pathway. In the foreground, an elderly couple is strolling leisurely, while in the background, dense green foliage can be observed. The surrounding elements, such as wooden benches and blooming flowers, help provide additional context. Judging by the golden lighting and long shadows, it appears that it is late afternoon, creating a sense of tranquility and peace.”

Example 4: A Business Meeting (Professional)

“This well-composed image captures a scene set in a modern corporate office, with the main focus on a team of professionals. In the foreground, a man in a suit is gesturing expressively while speaking, while in the background, a presentation screen can be observed. The surrounding elements, such as glasses of water and notepads, help provide additional context. Judging by the attentive body language of the colleagues, it appears that they are discussing a crucial project, creating a sense of collaboration and professionalism.”

Example 5: A Restaurant/Café (Social)

“This well-composed image captures a scene set in a cozy café, with the main focus on two individuals sitting at a table. In the foreground, they are holding coffee cups and smiling, while in the background, blurred patrons and baristas can be observed. The surrounding elements, such as warm lighting and rustic decor, help provide additional context. Judging by their cheerful demeanor, it appears that they are enjoying a casual conversation, creating a sense of warmth and friendship.”
